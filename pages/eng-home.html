About Us

Cleaning company "Lviv Pearl" is the embodiment of a dream of a team of professionals with many years of experience in cleaning residential and commercial premises. We have worked in different formats — from private orders to servicing large facilities, and this allowed us to form our own approach to quality, service and customer care.

Our company represents modern cleaning methods, eco-friendly products and European equipment. We carefully selected tools, staff and standards so that every cleaning is not just clean but flawless. We work in Lviv and the Lviv region, helping people live in comfort, coziness and cleanliness every day.

  • Offices, commercial and public premises
  • General and regular cleaning
  • Cleaning after renovation and events
  • Furniture and carpet dry cleaning
